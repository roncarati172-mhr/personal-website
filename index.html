About

Adam Smith is a visual artist exploring the intersection of traditional and contemporary art forms. Through a minimalistic approach, each piece seeks to evoke emotion and provoke thought.

Working across various mediums including painting, digital art, and mixed media, the work reflects a deep appreciation for form, color, and the spaces between.
